# 🗺 Viaje Oaxaca → Ciudad de Guatemala

An interactive road trip map covering a 3-week journey through southern Mexico and Guatemala, starting in Oaxaca City and ending in Guatemala City with a return flight to Mexico City.

## Route

**Oaxaca City → Tehuantepec → Tuxtla Gutiérrez → San Cristóbal de las Casas → La Mesilla (border) → Huehuetenango → Quetzaltenango (Xela) → Antigua Guatemala → Guatemala City ✈️ Mexico City**

~1,200 km · 9 stops · 1 border crossing · ~22 days

## Highlights

| Region | Highlights |
|--------|-----------|
| 🇲🇽 **Oaxaca** | Monte Albán ruins, Hierve el Agua, mezcal distilleries (Matatlán), UNESCO cuisine |
| 🇲🇽 **Chiapas** | Cañón del Sumidero, San Juan Chamula, Agua Azul, Palenque, San Cristóbal colonial city |
| 🇬🇹 **Guatemala** | Fuentes Georginas hot springs, Acatenango volcano hike, Lake Atitlán, Antigua |

## Interactive Map

👉 **[Open the interactive map](https://vtesniere.github.io/viaje_oaxaca/)**

The map includes:
- Actual road routes fetched in real time via OpenStreetMap routing
- Click any stop to see highlights and their travel time from the base
- Click any highlight to show the exact driving route on the map
- Hover over any route segment for driving conditions and time estimates

## Travel Modes

The map supports three modes, switchable at the top of the sidebar:

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 🚗 **Car** | Full road trip — maximum flexibility for day trips |
| 🚌 **Bus** | ADO/OCC in Mexico, Pullman de Morales in Guatemala — cheaper, less flexible |
| 🔀 **Hybrid** | Rent car Oaxaca→San Cristóbal, tourist shuttles for the Guatemala leg |

## Key Planning Notes

- All driving segments are under 6 h/day
- Cross La Mesilla border before 10 am
- San Cristóbal de las Casas is at 2,200 m — pack a jacket
- Fill up with fuel before entering Guatemala (cheaper in Mexico)
- Return flight: La Aurora Airport (GUA) → Mexico City (MEX/NLU)
